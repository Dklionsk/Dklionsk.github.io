Nine Easy Pieces (Solution)
The mechanic here is pretty simple: Each clue corresponds to the number. Index the numbers into letters and you get the answer, ARGONAUTS.
  • A 1 The loneliest number
  • R 18 0x12
  • G 7 Years in Tibet
  • O 15 Value of SQUID in Scrabble
  • N 14 Academy Award Nominations for Titanic (1997)
  • A 1 Alef
  • U 21 Starring Jim Sturgess, Kevin Spacey and Laurence Fishburne
  • T 20 Faces on an icosahedron
  • S 19 The eighth smallest prime number
